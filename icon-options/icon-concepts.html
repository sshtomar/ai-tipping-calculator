Geometric Construction Reference

All icons are built on a 1024×1024 grid. The T letterform uses consistent proportions: horizontal bar width = ~39% of canvas, stroke width = ~7.6%, corner radius = ~1%. Shown at 180pt (App Store), 80pt (home screen), and 40pt (Spotlight).
